## Understanding the Exam Pattern

The Prelims consists of two papers:
- **Paper I (General Studies)**: 200 marks
- **Paper II (CSAT)**: 200 marks (Qualifying with 33% marks)

## Subject-Wise Preparation Strategy

### 1. History and Culture
- Focus on Ancient, Medieval, and Modern Indian History
- Art and Culture: NCERT Class 11 (An Introduction to Indian Art)
- Important: Freedom Struggle and Constitutional Development

### 2. Geography
- Physical Geography: NCERT Classes 11 and 12
- Indian Geography: Resource distribution, climate, vegetation
- World Geography: Major geographical phenomena

### 3. Polity and Governance
- Indian Constitution by DD Basu or M. Laxmikanth
- Current affairs related to governance and constitutional issues
- Focus on fundamental rights, DPSPs, and amendments

### 4. Economy
- NCERT Classes 11 and 12
- Economic Survey and Union Budget highlights
- Current economic issues and government schemes

### 5. Environment and Ecology
- NCERT Class 12 Biology (Chapters on Environment)
- Current environmental issues and climate change
- Government initiatives and international agreements

### 6. Current Affairs
- Daily newspaper reading (The Hindu/Indian Express)
- Monthly current affairs compilation
- Integration with static subjects

## Time Management Tips

1. **Create a realistic study schedule**: Allocate time based on your strengths and weaknesses
2. **Regular revision**: Minimum 3 revisions before the exam
3. **Mock tests**: Solve at least 20-25 full-length tests
4. **Previous year papers**: Analyze last 10 years' papers

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Ignoring CSAT preparation
- Not practicing enough mock tests
- Studying too many sources
- Neglecting revision
- Poor time management during the exam

## Final Preparation Strategy

### Three Months Before Exam
- Complete syllabus coverage
- Start intensive revision
- Solve previous year papers

### One Month Before Exam
- Focus on revision only
- Daily mock tests
- Current affairs consolidation

### Last Week
- Light revision of notes
- Stay calm and confident
- Maintain health and sleep schedule
